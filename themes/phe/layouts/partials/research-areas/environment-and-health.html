Environment and Health

The Centre for Radiation, Chemical and Environmental Hazards (CRCE) is part of the PHE Health Protection and Medical Directorate and is the focal point for independent advice on health risks from exposure to:

  • Radiation
  • Chemicals
  • Environmental change
  • Extreme weather events such as flooding
Specialist areas
  • Toxicology of chemicals
  • Climate change and extreme events
  • Radiation
